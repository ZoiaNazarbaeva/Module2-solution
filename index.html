Our Menu

Chicken

For these delicious recipes, we carefully wrap tall, tender pieces of Chicken in a crispy coating, and cook them at the perfect temperature to retain all of their flavour. Crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside: everything you need in your life.

Beef

Our best-in-county beef roasted with shack seasoning, dipped in jus, and topped with hot or sweet peppers. Our legendary beef covered in a breathtaking blend of glorious cheeses.

Fish

Aqui Há Peixe means “there’s fish in here” and to honor the name grilled, cooked or roasted fish is the most important and special ingredient. Seafood is also available for those who want to be accompanied with the truest taste of the sea.
